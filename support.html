Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe to Appetizer Premium?

To subscribe:

  1. Open the Appetizer app
  2. Tap on your profile icon
  3. Select "Upgrade to Premium"
  4. Choose monthly or yearly plan
  5. Complete purchase through Apple

You'll get a 3-day free trial to try all premium features!

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Appetizer
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

Try these steps:

  1. Force close the app and reopen it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check for app updates in the App Store
  4. Ensure you have the latest iOS version
  5. If problem persists, contact support

Subscription not working

If your subscription isn't active:

  1. Check your subscription status in iPhone Settings
  2. Ensure payment method is valid
  3. Try logging out and back in
  4. Restore purchases: Profile ‚Üí Restore Purchases
  5. Contact us with your purchase receipt

Recipes not loading

If recipes won't load:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Try switching between WiFi and cellular data
  3. Close and reopen the app
  4. Clear app cache: Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Clear Cache

Ingredient scanner not working

For scanner issues:

  1. Grant camera permission: Settings ‚Üí Appetizer ‚Üí Camera
  2. Ensure good lighting
  3. Clean your camera lens
  4. Try scanning one ingredient at a time
  5. Update to the latest app version

Refund Policy

All purchases are processed through Apple. To request a refund:

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your Appetizer purchase
  4. Select "Request a refund"
  5. Choose a reason and submit

Apple will review and process your request. Refund decisions are made by Apple, not Appetizer.
