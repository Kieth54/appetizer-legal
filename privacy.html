Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

Welcome to Appetizer. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address - Used for account creation and authentication
  • Name - Optional, may be provided through Apple Sign In
  • User ID - Automatically generated unique identifier

1.2 User Content

We store content you create within the app:

  • Saved recipes and recipe collections
  • Meal plans and shopping lists
  • Chat conversations with our AI assistant
  • Dietary preferences and restrictions
  • Recipe search history within the app
  • Bookmarks and favorites

1.3 Subscription Information

  • Subscription status (active, trial, expired)
  • Subscription type (monthly or yearly)
  • Purchase receipts (for validation only)

1.4 Usage Information

We collect limited usage data to improve the app:

  • Features you use within the app
  • App performance data (crashes, errors)
  • Device type and operating system version
What We DON'T Collect:
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Contacts or address book
  • ❌ Photos (except when you explicitly scan ingredients)
  • ❌ Browsing history outside the app
  • ❌ Advertising identifiers
  • ❌ Health or fitness data
  • ❌ Financial information (handled securely by Apple)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service - Create and manage your account, save your recipes and preferences
  • Personalization - Deliver personalized recipe recommendations based on your preferences
  • AI Features - Process your requests to our AI recipe assistant
  • Subscription Management - Manage your premium subscription and validate purchases
  • Customer Support - Respond to your questions and support requests
  • App Improvement - Analyze usage patterns to improve features and fix bugs
  • Security - Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance - Comply with applicable laws and regulations
We will NEVER:
  • ❌ Sell your personal data to third parties
  • ❌ Use your data for advertising purposes
  • ❌ Share your recipes or meal plans with other users without your permission
  • ❌ Send you marketing emails without your consent

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Service Providers

We share your information with trusted service providers who help us operate the app:

  • Supabase - Database hosting and authentication (encrypted storage)
  • Apple - Payment processing for subscriptions
  • Google AI (Gemini) - Recipe generation (we send only recipe requests, not personal data)

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or security issues
  • Protect the safety of our users

3.3 Business Transfers

If Appetizer is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred to the new owner.

4. Data Security

We take security seriously and implement industry-standard measures:

  • Encryption - All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Secure Authentication - Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Apple Sign In - Option for privacy-enhanced authentication
  • Access Controls - Strict access controls to your data
  • Regular Updates - We regularly update our security practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your information within the app:

  • View and edit your profile
  • Update dietary preferences
  • Manage saved recipes and meal plans

5.2 Delete Your Data

You can delete your data by:

  • Deleting individual recipes, meal plans, or chat history within the app
  • Contacting us to delete your entire account

5.3 Export Your Data

You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at thabanichaka@gmail.com

5.4 Opt-Out of Communications

You can opt out of promotional emails by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email we send.

5.5 Cancel Subscription

You can cancel your subscription anytime through your Apple ID account settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Appetizer is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using Appetizer, you consent to the transfer of your information to our servers and service providers.

8. Third-Party Links

Appetizer may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending you an email notification (for material changes)
  • Displaying a notice in the app

Your continued use of Appetizer after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at thabanichaka@gmail.com

11. European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Our legal basis for processing your data is your consent and our legitimate business interests.
